About us

With 30 years of legacy in creating leaders, Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) now brings to you PICT Model School and Nestling Day-care. These futuristic educational establishments have been meticulously designed and planned to offer one of its kind learning experience to the 21st century kids.

Vision

To impart education that fosters global citizenship with values deep rooted in our culture and providing learning opportunities to tap potential of every individual in a stimulating environment, where tradition meets technology.

Mission

To provide stimulating and innovative tools and techniques through modern infrastructure, befitting individual learning needs and empowering them to meet the challenges of education, work and life in a rapidly changing global scenario.

Our Activities

  • Independence Day
  • Janmashtami
  • Field Trip
  • Role Play
  • Strokes & Shapes

Students at PICT Model School were dressed for the occasion of Independence Day, and were present in school early morning for the unfurling of the national flag. This was followed by the salute, national anthem and then the patriotic songs.

Festivals are an important part of the Indian culture, and at PICT, we make sure that the children are aware of the importance of every festival while they have fun celebrating it. On the occasion of Janmashtami, children were all dressed in their best ethnic wears and practised the tradition of breaking the pot on this day.

At PICT Model School, children are taken on field trips where they enjoy as well as gain a lot of knowledge. This field trip was taken to the Katraj Zoo where the children saw animals and gained information about each one of them.

Another fun way of gaining knowledge is role play. On this day children chose their own characters and dressed themselves likewise and spoke about their chosen character. This help them know their character as well as have fun using acting and vocal skills.

Co-curriculum is a big part of PICT Model School as we believe in learning by doing. This activity called Strokes and Shapes was organized for children to help them draw and colour giving a boost to their creative minds.
